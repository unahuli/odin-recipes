Cream of Vegetable Soup

Description

Do you have leftover vegetables in the fridge? Need to cook something quick and easy? Try our Cream of Vegetable Soup recipe for a delicious meal that will save you time and money.

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3 cans (10-1/2 ounces each)
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups half-and-half cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • condensed chicken broth, undiluted, or 4 cups chicken broth
  • 5 cups chopped leftover cooked mixed vegetables (such as broccoli, carrots and cauliflower)

Steps:

  1. In a Dutch oven, saute onion in butter until tender.
  2. Add flour; stir until blended. Gradually add broth. Bring to a boil.
  3. Cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened.
  4. Stir in the milk, cream, basil, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
  5. Add the vegetables; cook gently until heated through.
