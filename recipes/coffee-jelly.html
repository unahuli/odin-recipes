Coffee Jelly

Description

A famous dessert in Japan during summer. Coffee mixed with gelatin cubed, chilled and can be served with whipped cream.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (.25 ounce) package unflavored gelatin
  • 2 tablespoons hot water
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 cups fresh brewed coffee

Steps:

  1. Dissolve gelatin in the hot water in a small bowl.
  2. Pour gelatin mixture, coffee, and sugar in a saucepan and bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Pour coffee mixture into glasses for individual servings or a large pan for cubing.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator until solidified, 6 to 7 hours.
